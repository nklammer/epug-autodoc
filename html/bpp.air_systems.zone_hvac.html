write_to_os(model)#

Method to write the WaterSourceHeatPump to OpenStudio.

Creates heat pump cooling coil, sets its COP, and adds it to a demand branch of the condenser loop. Creates heat pump heating coil, sets its COP, and adds it to a demand branch of the condenser loop. Creates backup electric heating coil. Creates on-off type fan. Creates AirTerminalSingleDuctInletSideMixer and adds branch to airloop w/ just mixer. Adds zone and new mixer to a branch. Creates ZoneHVACWaterToAirHeatPump and fills it with heating coil, cooling coil, backup heating coil, and heat pump fan. Adds the WSHP to zone and mixer_outlet_node.
