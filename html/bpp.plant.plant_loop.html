class bpp.plant.plant_loop.PlantLoop(id='23efa76c-2e59-459d-888b-e9d68668f884')#

Bases: object

An object class to represent plant loops.

Plant loops can be for delivering heating or cooling but are also used for domestic hot water use inside the building. Subtypes of PlantLoop: heating plant loop, chiller plant loop, condenser loop, DHW loop

Parameters:
idstr, optional

The UUID of the object.
