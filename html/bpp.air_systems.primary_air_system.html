Writes the PackagedSingleZone object to OpenStudio.

For each zone in zone_list creates an AirLoopHVAC in the model. Sets appropriate options for sizing run. Creates cooling coil. Creates heating coil and backup heating coil if appropriate. Creates AirLoopHVACUnitarySystem to hold the coils and the fan. Sets the controlling thermostat to the zone level. Creates an AirTerminal and assigns to zone.

Parameters:
modelopenstudio.model.model object
Returns:
packaged_single_zone_systemslist of openstudio.model.AirLoopHVAC objects
